Replace the placeholder filenames below with your own images.

Suggested workflow:
- Drop your image files into `/public/photos`
- Update the single `photos` array in `/content/photos.ts`
- The first image in that array becomes the hero image automatically
- The full array is used for the gallery automatically

Tips:
- Vertical or slightly portrait-leaning images work beautifully on mobile.
- Keep hero image at high quality, ideally 1600px wide or larger.
- Update captions or filenames in `/content/photos.ts` if you want different image names.
